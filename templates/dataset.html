About the Dataset

This dataset is custom-built to train a machine learning model for music mood prediction. Songs are collected from Spotify mood-based playlists, ensuring diverse genres and emotions.

Using the Librosa library, key audio features are extracted from each song, including:

  • Energy – Measures intensity and liveliness.
  • Loudness – Determines how soft or loud a song is.
  • Tempo – The speed or pace of a song (BPM).
  • Valence – Indicates positivity or negativity of a song's mood.

Based on these features, songs are categorized into moods such as Happy, Sad, Energetic, and Calm. This dataset plays a crucial role in training a model to accurately predict the mood of any given song.
